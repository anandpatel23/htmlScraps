Celldweller

Celldweller is a Detroit, Michigan-based Electronic Rock project that was created by multi-instrumentalist Klayton, whose real name is Scott Albert, former frontman and songwriter of the bands Circle of Dust, Argyle Park, Angeldust (with Criss Angel), and many more. Celldweller's music is multi-faceted, often labeled electronic rock and more commonly being described as a combination of rock and trance. Celldweller songs have frequently appeared in movies, trailers, television shows, and video games.
