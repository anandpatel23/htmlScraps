Camo & Krooked

Camo & Krooked are a Drum & Bass duo from Vienna, Austria consisting of Reinhard Rietsch (aka Camo, Born November 12, 1983 in Salzburg) and Markus Wagner (aka Krooked, Born July 31, 1989 in Lilienfeld)
